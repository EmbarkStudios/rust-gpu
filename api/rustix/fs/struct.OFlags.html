Similar to ACCMODE, but just includes the read/write flags, and no other flags.

Some implementations include O_PATH in O_ACCMODE, when sometimes we really just want the read/write bits. Caution is indicated, as the presence of O_PATH may mean that the read/write bits donâ€™t have their usual meaning.
